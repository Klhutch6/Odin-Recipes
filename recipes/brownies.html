Hutch's delicious brownies

Description

The following recipe will make a delicious batch of brownies for all to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, canola oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs. A good rule of thumb is better early than late with brownies.
